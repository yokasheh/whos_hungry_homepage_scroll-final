Start a meetup with your friends.

Want to meet up with friends for a quick bite to eat in between classes? Tired of eating dinner at your apartment with your roommates?

Invite your krew

With our app, you don't even need to have the app to participate, you can invite friends from your contacts with a single click and they'll receive a text inviting them to download the app and join you in your adventures. They'll even get a follow up text summarizing the meetup after the voting has finished!

Vote on where to go

Simply vote up for where you want to go, and down for where you don't!

Watch it all go down

Say goodybe to the classic "Hey, I'm here when will you be here?" text or call. 20 minutes before the meetup, your location pops up in an Uber or Lyft type of interface where you can see everyone as they approach the restaurant.
